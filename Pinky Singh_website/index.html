Let's Talk About AI Evolution

1950s — The Origin of AI

The first AI concepts were born, focused on simple logic and problem-solving systems.

1980s — Expert Systems Era

Computers started making human-like decisions through rule-based knowledge systems.

2000s — Machine Learning Growth

AI began learning from large datasets, improving accuracy and performance.

2012 — Deep Learning Breakthrough

Neural networks advanced rapidly, enabling machines to understand images, speech, and patterns with high precision.

2022 — Generative AI Takes Over

Models like ChatGPT, Midjourney, and DALL·E turned AI into a creative tool capable of generating text, images, music, and even video.

Today — AI Everywhere

Generative AI is now embedded in daily life, powering innovation across education, business, healthcare, entertainment, and more.
